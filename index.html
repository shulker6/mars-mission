Oxygen production
Mars's 95% CO₂ atmosphere fuels cyanobacteria inside bioreactors — turning astronauts' exhaled CO₂ into breathable O₂, replacing heavy consumable tanks entirely.
Water recycling
NASA's MELiSSA project uses bacteria to recover up to 95% of wastewater biologically — eliminating the need to launch tonnes of water from Earth on a multi-year mission.
Growing food
Martian regolith is toxic to plants. Rhizobium, Azotobacter, and mycorrhizal fungi together fix nitrogen, neutralize perchlorates, and transform sterile soil into farmland.
